"Mission: Colonizer"
Mission Parameters: You have been hired on by Colonial Standards Inc (CS) to travel to and eventually
colonize an alien planet. You will be tasked with testing the habitability of the planet by colonizing
and farming the local flora and fauna. To determine the survivability of this planet, you must survive
off of the land for a total of N days. You will have access to CS's Universal Credit Transaction System
to purchase equipment needed to help in your endeavor.
